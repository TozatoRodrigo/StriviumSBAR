# Screenshots Guide

Use only fictitious data in all store screenshots.

## Required Screens

- Sign-in screen with Strivium Link branding.
- Workspace selection or workspace home.
- Hospitalizations list with fictitious patients.
- Patient/hospitalization detail screen.
- Clinical evolution creation or review screen.
- Team or doctors management screen if available to the demo account.

## Apple Sizes

Capture at least:

- iPhone 6.7-inch display.
- iPhone 6.5-inch display if required by App Store Connect.
- iPad screenshots only if iPad distribution remains enabled.

## Google Play Assets

Capture:

- Phone screenshots.
- Feature graphic.
- App icon and adaptive icon preview.

## Review Rules

- Do not show real patient, hospital, physician, CRM, email, phone, document, or attachment data.
- Do not show experimental AI/voice dictation screens in v1 while the flags are disabled.
- Keep captions factual and avoid diagnostic, treatment, or regulatory claims.
